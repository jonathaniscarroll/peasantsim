PeasantSim

You are a worker in the technofeudal wasteland of the gig economy. Set in a privatized, post-state technocracy of international corporate rights fostered by powerful lobbyists, PeasantSim gives you virtual gigs to move through physical space overlaid by Augmented Reality. You are tasked with the impossible: completing your gigs before they run out of value as an erstwhile resource for corporate data-miners.

PeasantSim is being developed as a response to questions raised by the obfuscated ‘public’ process connected to Sidewalk Labs techno-utopian proposal to revitalize Toronto’s waterfront-- an exercise in urban data accumulation that threatens to become a newly exploited commons for our real life technofeudal overlords, Google.

PeasantSim provokes conversations about spaces, technology, and labour in the face of ever-encroaching corporate reach.
